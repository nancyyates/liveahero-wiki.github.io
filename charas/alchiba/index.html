Personal Data 1

如何なる事件も「白黒つける」という彼の信念は、漆黒の翼の色すら白く染め上げ、彼を純白の羽毛を誇る探偵ヒーローへと変身させる。装備している片眼鏡のパラレルウェポンは、対象が秘匿する情報のすべてをスキャンして調書として視覚化する能力を持っており、その調書を読み取り分析・推理する事によって、相手の素性や弱点、発生している事件の真相すらも白日の下に晒す事が出来る。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

Alchiba has the belief that “finding the truth in black and white” is most important, so he dyes even his jet-black wings white, transforming him into a detective hero. His Parallel weapon, the monocle, has the ability to scan all the information hidden by the subject and visualize it as a record, and by reading, analyzing and inferring the record, the enemy’s features and weaknesses, even the truth of the incident that is occurring can come to light.

Personal Data 2

とある国家警察の内部に於いて、警察官であった彼は暴いてはいけない内情を暴き、その腐敗を糾弾したことにより、冤罪を着せられた上で職を追われることになった。以来彼は警察という組織に対して深い不信感を抱いており、自らの手で事件に「白黒つける」ことに拘るようになっていった。彼の目の前に白黒つけるべき事件が存在し続ける限り、彼は探偵兼ヒーローとして活躍し続けるだろう。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

He was a police officer in the national police, but he was falsely accused of uncovering the secrets that would reveal a large corruption. Since then, he has been deeply distrustful of the police organization and has become obsessed with “black and white”. As long as there is a black-and-white incident in front of him, he will continue to be a detective and hero.

Personal Data 1

知識だけではなく、力を以て事件の真相を暴き出す「武装探偵」の姿を得たアルキバ。文化遺産の破壊テロに対抗するために、冷静かつ鋭い思考で推理し、熱い情熱と右手のレーザー銃で鎮圧に掛かる。その銃は警察時代に手にしていた物に似ており、皮肉だろうと嘲笑しながらも、手に馴染んだトリガーへと指を掛ける。そして、機動性の高い新たな姿でテントテレスの砂原を翔けていく。

Personal Data 1 (Translated)

Alchiba gained the appearance of an “armed detective” who seeks to uncover the truth of the case with power as well as knowledge.

He infers with calm and keen thoughts to counter the terrorist destruction of cultural heritage, and squeezes the trigger of his laser gun with righteous fury.

The gun resembles what he had in his police days, striking him as bitterly ironic, noting the painfully familiar feeling as it fits perfectly in his hand.

So, he will fly through the sandy dunes of Ten Teres with a new, highly mobile form.

Personal Data 2

新たに得た姿では変身後も白ではなく、黒の翼を身に纏っている。これは彼自身のあるがままの姿の象徴であり、「不幸を呼ぶ黒い翼」と称された過去から逃げ出さず、トラウマと化した警察官時代の境遇をその翼で乗り越えた証拠である。智と武の両方を手にした今、アルキバは目を背けてきた警察という組織を見つめ直す。不信感はまだ拭い切れてはいないが、警察に対する彼の想いにも変化の兆しが見え始めている。

Personal Data 2 (Translated)

In this new form, he wears black wings instead of white even after transformation.

This is a symbol of his old self, the officer past he was never able to put behind him as “the black wings that bring misery.”

Now that he has gained both wisdom and power, Alchiba looks back at the police organization that he turned away from years ago.

His distrust hasn’t been wiped out yet, but his feelings for the police are beginning to show signs of change.

Side Story

探偵事務所を営む私立探偵の有翼宇宙人。自称「ニヒルでクールな二枚目探偵」。切れ長の目に甘いマスクの顔立ちを有するが、本性は依頼人すら口説こうとする軟派なプレイボーイであり、スケベな上に欲深い。また、ペラペラと歯の浮くようなセリフを発する。しかしながら「受けた依頼は死んでも達成する」という信条を掲げているだけあって、仕事の完璧さには定評がある為、探偵業は存外盛況らしい。

Side Story (Translated)

A winged alien who runs a detective bureau.

Because of his good-looking facial features and captivating narrow eyes, he’s a self-proclaimed “nihilistic and cool” handsome detective. In reality, he’s a flirty playboy who tries to seduce even his own clients, showing just how lecherous and greedy he is.

He tends to speak incessant bickering that could set anyone’s teeth on edge. Nevertheless, he firmly follows his creed that “He will fulfill any requests recieved even if he dies in the process”. This is because of his established reputation for laboral perfection, which has the detective industry thriving beyond expectation.

Notes

  • His Hero lines refers to Gammei
  • Alchiba is named after the fourth brightest star, also known as Alpha Corvi, in the constellation Corvus or the Crow.
  • His skill name is from Greek language:
    • Pista Epidiosi (πίστα επιδίωξη) : Track Pursuit
    • Vasanistírio (βασανιστήριο) : Torture/torment
    • Mávro Áspro (μαύρο άσπρο) : Mávro is Black, Áspro is White
    • Lepída Steréosis (λεπίδα στερέωσις) : Lepída is Blade, Steréosis is Fasten
    • Sféra Katadíki̱ (σφαίρα καταδίκη) : Sféra means “bullet, sphere” and Katadíki̱ means “judgment, judge”.
