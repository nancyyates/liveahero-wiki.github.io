Event Quest: New Year 2021

New Year 2021
Duration
Main Quests 1 Jan 2021 00:00 JST to 15 Jan 2021 14:00 JST

Event Details

Lifewonders is launching their first new year campaign, together with the event there will be a special scenario “To an unforgettable year”.

Completion of the scenario will give you one エーテルチケット.
