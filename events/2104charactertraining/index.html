Event Quest: Character Training Campaign

Character Training Campaign
Duration
Main Quests 14 Apr 2021 18:00 JST to 23 Apr 2021 14:00 JST
News link

Event Details

For a limited time there will be special training quest, consisting of two ranks (A and B) with three kinds of rewards (Exp, Laf, Training Band) for a total of 6 quests.

  • [A Rank] Hero Training!
  • [B Rank] Hero Training!
  • [A Rank] Hero Training!
  • [B Rank] Hero Training!
  • [A Rank] Hero Training!
  • [B Rank] Hero Training!

Each first clear will grant 1 Ether Stone.

Also, there will be daily missions and limited time missions for character training campaign, rewarding エーテルチケット and レジェンドメダル.

Lastly, together with this campaign we will get our next challenge quest, which is A Challenge From the Fisherman (Isaribi), this challenge quest will have 4 quests and clearing all of them will rewards 1 エーテルチケット.
