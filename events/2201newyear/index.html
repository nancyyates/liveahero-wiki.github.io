  • “Water Bullet” Akashi
  • “The Chaser” Alchiba
  • “Cool Gunfighter” Barrel
  • “Active Agent” Kyoichi
  • “The Adventurer” Marfik
  • Nessen
  • “Drunken Tiger” Ryekie
  • “Dynamic Rescue” Victom

You can only pull in this Ether Search once and with 50 paid エーテル晶石
