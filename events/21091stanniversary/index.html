Event Quest: 1st Anniversary

1st Anniversary
Duration
Main Quests 30 Sep 2021 20:00 JST to 14 Oct 2021 23:59 JST
News link

Live A Hero is about to celebrate its first anniversary since release. The 1st Anniversary Campaign will be held from 20:00 JST (scheduled) on Thursday, September 30, 2021.

Campaign Details
